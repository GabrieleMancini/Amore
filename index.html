The Sound

Italian groove.
Made to dance.

Amore is a wild, vibrant slice of Mediterranean life bursting into unexpected spaces across Hamburg. We host high-energy gatherings rooted in a deep, curated love for the Italian sound—from Neapolitan funk to timeless Italo disco.

We start with an Aperitivo, but as the room heats up and the colors get louder, the energy explodes into a full-blown dancefloor. Unpretentious, loud, colorful, and made for people who seek genuine warmth and love to live fully.

The Vibe

Good music, crazy people.

We transform spaces. A room full of friends, massive speakers, colorful decor, and a DJ who knows how to make you sweat.

The music starts groovy—vintage funk, sunny anthems—and escalates into house, dance, and pure madness. No strict rules, just pure Amore.

  • From Funk to MadnessThe journey is everything. We start with a vintage record and end up in a rave.
  • Wild SpacesTransformed courtyards, rooftops, and warehouses dressed up in color.
