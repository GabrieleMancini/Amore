AMORE

The classic Italian afternoon. Hamburg's Wildest Fiesta
The Sound

Italian groove.
Made to dance.

Amore is a wild, vibrant slice of Mediterranean life bursting into unexpected spaces across Hamburg. We host high-energy gatherings rooted in a deep, curated love for the Italian sound—from Neapolitan funk to timeless Italo disco.

We start with an elegant Aperitivo, but as the room heats up and the colors get louder, the energy explodes into a full-blown dancefloor. Chic, colorful, and made for people who seek genuine warmth and love to live fully.

The Records

Italian vinyl, dug deep. Funk, disco, and heavy grooves that make you sweat with style.

The Places

Elegant spaces transformed into surreal, festive Mediterranean escapes.

The Crowd

Wild, beautiful souls. People who let loose but keep it classy.
